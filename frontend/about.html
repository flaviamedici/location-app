About OutTogether

OutTogether is a friendly community app that helps people discover others nearby who share similar interests, hobbies, and lifestyles.

Our Mission

We believe meaningful connections can start with a simple introduction. Our goal is to make it easier for neighbors and local communities to meet.

What You Can Do

  • Explore people near you on the map
  • Filter by interests and location
  • Discover shared hobbies and communities

Why It Exists

Whether you’re new to a city or simply want to connect with people nearby, OutTogether makes local discovery feel simple, welcoming, and useful.
